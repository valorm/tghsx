Protocol Governance

Our commitment to credible neutrality and decentralization. Learn about the current governance structure and our path toward a community-led future.

Current State: MVP Phase (Centralized Control)

In the current Minimum Viable Product (MVP) phase, the tGHSX protocol is managed by a single `owner` account. This centralized approach is a temporary and necessary measure designed to ensure stability, enable rapid iteration based on user feedback, and guarantee security during the earliest stages of development.

The `owner` role has significant privileges, including the ability to upgrade system parameters and pause the contract in an emergency. We believe in full transparency about these risks and are actively working to transition these powers to a decentralized governance framework as outlined in our roadmap.

The Future: Decentralized, Community-Led Governance

Our ultimate goal is for the tGHSX protocol to be governed entirely by its community. This involves a phased transition from the current owner-controlled model to a robust, decentralized autonomous organization (DAO).

In this future state, token holders will be able to propose, debate, and vote on all changes to the protocol, including:

  • Adjusting risk parameters like collateralization ratios and stability fees.
  • Adding new types of collateral.
  • Allocating treasury funds for ecosystem development.
  • Upgrading smart contracts.

This transition ensures that the protocol remains a credibly neutral piece of financial infrastructure that serves its users, not a central entity.

Governance Roadmap

Phase 1: MVP In Progress

  • Core protocol development & deployment
  • Public testnet on Amoy
  • Community feedback gathering via Discord & GitHub

Phase 2: Mainnet & Audits Planned

  • Third-party security audits
  • Mainnet launch with `owner` control
  • Expanded documentation and guides

Phase 3: Decentralization Planned

  • Introduction of a governance token
  • Launch of the tGHSX DAO
  • Transition of `owner` privileges to the DAO
