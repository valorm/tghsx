Frequently Asked Questions

What is tGHSX?
+
tGHSX is a synthetic stablecoin pegged to the Ghanaian Cedi (GHS), built on the Polygon blockchain. It allows users to deposit crypto collateral (like ETH) and borrow a stable token without selling their assets.
How is the peg to the GHS maintained?
+
The peg is maintained through a system of over-collateralization and market incentives. Every tGHSX is backed by more than its value in ETH, and the protocol uses Chainlink oracles for real-time price feeds to ensure vaults remain solvent. Arbitrage opportunities help keep the market price close to the peg.
